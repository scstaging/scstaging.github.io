Architextural™ Screen & Post System

The Ikonik 2020 Architextural™ Screen Collection offers a myriad of possibilities suited to both interior and exterior applications. Our standard and custom designed screens artfully define living spaces and transitional areas. Whether you seek to establish privacy or shade, create stunning feature walls or dramatic stand-alone art works--there is an Ikonik solution.

Engineering Solution

Max. wind gust of 130kph assuming a solid panel / Load of 0.5kPa per OBC 4.1.5.16 (wall used as guard) / UDL of 0.75kN/m or 1.0kN point load applied at 42” per OBC 4.1.5.14 (1) (c)

Technical Specification

72 Series Screen (Aluminum) - 36”x72” 10 gauge (0.1019“) powder-coated alumimum 72 Series Screen (Steel) - 36”x72” 14 gauge (0.0747”) powder-coated steel 88 Series Screen (Aluminum) - 44”x88” 10 gauge (0.1019”) powder-coated aluminum 88 Series Screen (Steel) - 44”x88” 14 gauge (0.0747”) powder-coated steel *Standard architectural screens include a 1.5” structural flange along all edges **Select screens available as dimmable LED backlit models (contact Ikonik for details)

Architextural™ Post Specification


72 & 88 Series Post (Alum) - 4”x4“x3/16” (Wall) Ikonik Karbon Black powder-coated alum. tubing w/ welded baseplate

​72 & 88 Series Post (Steel) - 4”x4“x1/8” (Wall) Ikonik Karbon Black powder-coated steel tubing w/ welded baseplate
