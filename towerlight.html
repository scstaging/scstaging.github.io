Tower Light by Ikonik

At Ikonik we believe that successful design is rooted in psychology--articulating how the physical & aesthetic characteristics of an object impact those who come in contact with it. The Ikonik ‘80 Series’ Tower Light is an exploration in exagerated proportion, luminosity, and refined texture that inspires awe and lends a certain dignified opulence to your nightscape. We invite you to step into the light and discover a new era of exterior lighting.

Technical Specification

​• 80”(H) x 15”(DIA) 8 gauge (0.125”) laser cut/welded powder-coated aluminum shroud • Includes 0.25”(D) powder-coated aluminum baseplate & ‘dark sky’ compliant cover • Includes S/S assembly hardware and decorative caps (excludes lags, concrete anchors)
